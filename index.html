Samoyed Dog

Samoyed is one of the oldest breeds in the world and is very closely related to wolves.

  • Origin: Northern Russia and Siberia
  • Utilization: Sled and companion dog
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Average Height: 53 cm (female) to 57 cm (male)

Medium in size, elegant, white breed. In appearence gives the impression of power, charm and dignity. Has a happy expression which has earned the nickname "smiley dog". Very social and cannot be used as a guard dog. The hunting instinct is very slight.
